Flagship Initiatives (FY 2024-25)

Education

CCL Ke Lal & Laadli

A flagship scheme providing free residential coaching for IIT/NIT aspirants. 300+ students have succeeded so far. Recently, 5 alumni graduated from IIT-ISM Dhanbad, with one securing a Gold Medal.

₹ 1.5 Cr / Year Invested
Project Digital Vidya

Modernizing education with smart classrooms and computer labs in 193 government schools across 8 districts.

Smart Classrooms

Health & Nutrition

Nanha Sa Dil

A ₹9.54 Cr initiative with Sri Sathya Sai Trust. Screening 45,000 children for Congenital Heart Disease and funding 500 surgeries freely.

Centralized Kitchen

A massive ₹22.09 Cr kitchen in Ramgarh (with Akshaya Patra) to serve nutritious mid-day meals to 50,000 students in 538 schools.

Nikshay Mitra (TB Free)

Adopted TB patients in Latehar & Chatra. Providing nutrition baskets and monitoring to 5,140 patients. (Budget: ₹2.93 Cr)

Livelihood & Sustainability

Project Prakriti (Bunkar)

Empowering tribal women in Hazaribagh with natural dyeing & weaving skills. Income has tripled using local resources like Palash and Marigold.

E-Auto for Women

Provided electric rickshaws to 10 ST women, enabling them to earn ₹500-1000 daily and become self-reliant entrepreneurs.
