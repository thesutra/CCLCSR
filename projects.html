Flagship Initiatives (2024-25)

Health & Nutrition

Project RAKSHAK (Animal Welfare)

Jharkhand's first "Hospital on Wheels" for stray animals. Awarded Gold for Innovation. Treated 1,138+ animals.

Nanha Sa Dil

A comprehensive project for treating Congenital Heart Diseases (CHDs) in 4 districts of Jharkhand.

Education

CCL Ke Laal & Laali

Free engineering coaching for 40 meritorious students. 17 students qualified for JEE Mains in the 2022-24 batch.

State Library, Ranchi

Developing a 5,000-seater library at Ranchi University. Project cost: ₹65.25 Crore.

Sustainability

Project Prakriti (Handloom)

Empowering 75 Santhal tribal women in Tapin, Hazaribagh with natural dyeing and weaving training. Average income increased by ₹2,500-₹3,000.
