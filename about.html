About Our Mission

CSR Policy Framework

CCL's CSR initiatives are guided by the Companies Act 2013 and the DPE guidelines. Our policy mandates a minimum spend of 2% of the average net profit of the previous three financial years.

Key Focus Areas:

  • 80% of funds are utilized within a 25km radius of operational areas.
  • Emphasis on Aspirational Districts: Ranchi, Ramgarh, Chatra, Latehar, etc..
